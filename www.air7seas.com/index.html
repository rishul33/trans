Customs Release / Customs Clearance

Leave your worries of customs & government regulations, documentation & requirements on our knowledge & experienced staff. The clarity to regulations, their impact to carriers & others associated, options available to resolve or comply etc. are what we do. Customers customs brokers is licensed by the US customs and acts on customers related details.

The first step is to process the release of customer's goods through US customs-in most cases electronically, but sometimes manually depending on the carrier method of reporting. Your customs broker works with the carrier and customs to ensure the quick and seamless release of the goods.
